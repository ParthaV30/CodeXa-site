Frequently asked questions he who asks does not err

Hosting is a server, a place on the Internet for your website.

It allows you to reserve space for your website on someone else's server and put your work online. It's comparable to paying for a plot of land that someone rents to you.

The hosting price is not included in our quote

Purchasing hosting will be your responsibility, and we can help you with that. Once your website is hosted, we'll be able to implement any changes remotely, from your own computer, and we won't need your login details.

We offer free minor changes one year after the service contract ends.

It would be different if the changes were major, such as a new unique section or a complete overhaul of existing ones. In such cases, it takes significantly more time. However, all changes, such as replacing text, images, or small structural changes, will be free for one year.
